Mission 8: Steve's Attic

Steve was in his attic, when he noticed a piece of wood sticking through his roof. He wanted to know the measure of the angle of that wood, but the side whose measure he needed was inaccessible. The other side was 78 degrees. what is the unknown side?

Steve's roof forms a straight 180 degree angle, so anything dealing with it would be a complementary angle. (78 + x = 180) The angle he wants to know is 102 degrees.
