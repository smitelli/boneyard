Mission 5: Town Planning

When viewed from above, Almighty Dollar Road has a slope of 3. The planners want to run a new highway, Route 0, through the town. It has to cross Almighty Dollar Road at a right angle. What slope does Route 0 need?

The two roads have to be perpendicular, and perpendicular slopes have to equal -1 when multiplied. (3x = -1... Algebra.) Route 0 needs to have a slope of -1/3 to be perpendicular. The citizens can once again drive in joy, knowing that their roads meet at right angles.
