Mission 7: The Boat in the Garage

Wally has a boat. It's shaped like a regular pyramid with a square base. (A lot of weird people in this world, wouldn't you say?) The side of the base has a length of 10 feet, and the volume of the boat is 2600 cubic feet. Wally doesn't know how high his boat is, but he needs to fit it through an 8 foot high garage door. Can he do it?

The base area is (10 x 10 =) 100 square feet. Plugging these values into the "volume of a triangle" formula, we get 2600 = 1/3(100)h. Algebra... again! h comes out to be 78. Wally's boat is 78 feet high. I don't think it will fit in the garage without some serious structural modification.
