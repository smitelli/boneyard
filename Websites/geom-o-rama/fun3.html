Mission 3: This Old House

Eddie inherited an old house, but he's a little unsure about whether or not it would suit his needs. The house's bathtub is 66" x 25", and Eddie refuses to bathe unless the tub water is at least 11" deep. (He's weird.) A cubic inch of water weighs .2 pounds (as this is on a distant planet with different gravitational constants) and the house's safety inspector says the floor cannot support more than 3,000 pounds. Can Eddie accept this limit?

The water is a rectangular prism, and the volume is (66 x 25 x 11) = 18,150 cubic inches. If a cubic inch of water weighs .2 pounds, all the water weighs 3,630 pounds. Sadly, Eddie's house would surely collapse under this load.
