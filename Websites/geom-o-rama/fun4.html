Mission 4: Weeds on My Lawn

I noticed a perfectly square patch of crabgrass on my front lawn that measured 4" on one side. Two days later, I come back to the patch and find that the weeds have expanded to cover a square with 8" on each side. Being able to only communicate with my landscaper on mathematical terms, what is the scale factor of this growth?

This is simply a dilation. All of the sides doubled exactly. (8 / 4) = 2. The scale factor of this dilation is 2. simple, no?
