Mission 10: Right Triangle Question

Mark wants to know what the length of the hypotenuse is on a right triangle if the two legs have lengths of 2 and 5.

Well, Mark, it's called the Pythagorean Theorem. 22 + 52 = c2. The answer is the square root of 29, or about 5.39. It's simple, really.
