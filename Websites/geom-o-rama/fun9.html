Mission 9: Odd CD's

Moe bought a CD, but its dimensions were quite off. The CD itself had a radius of 35 cm, and the diameter of the center hole was 10 cm. What is the area of the disc?

The area of the large part is (pi)352, 3848.45 square cm. The area of the hole is (pi)52 (radius is half of the diameter... remember!) This works out to 78.54 square cm. Subtracting out the hole, the CD has an area of 3769.91 square cm. Quite odd, if I say so myself.
