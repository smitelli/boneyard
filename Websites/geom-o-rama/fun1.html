Mission 1: Car Trouble

Mister T. was driving his 1973 Ford Pinto along, when he stopped at a red light at the point (3, 5). Suddenly, the gas tank caught fire! Mister T. ran from his flaming motorcar, to point (-2, 7). Mister T. learned from listening to old Tom Lehrer albums that an exploding Pinto can hurl flaming shrapnel around in a 7-unit radius. Is Mister T. safe in his current spot?

No! This is a simple distance formula, so we need to find the distance between (3, 5) and (-2, 7). We plug it into the Distance Formula and get 5.39. Mister T. is standing about 5.39 units from the car. If the shrapnel can reach 7 units out, if would benefit Mister T. to move a bit farther from the car, to avoid personal injury.
